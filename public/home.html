Our Services

SIP Trunking

Seamless Integration: Connect your existing IP-PBX to the world. TalkUSA provides a robust SIP trunking solution compatible with major phone systems (3CX, Asterisk, Avaya, and more).
Pay-As-You-Go Efficiency: Stop paying for channels you don't use. Our elastic SIP trunks allow for unlimited concurrent calls while you only pay for the minutes you talk.
Reliability: Redundant network architecture ensures your business stays online.

Virtual Numbers (DID)

Local Presence, Everywhere: Establish a local footprint in cities across the USA without opening a physical office. Local numbers start at just $4.99/mo.
Toll-Free Credibility: Give your customers a free way to reach you and boost your brand's professional image. USA/CA Toll-free numbers start at $8.99/mo.
Inbound & Outbound: Full two-way voice capabilities for all your virtual numbers.

AI Agents (Voice & Video)

AI Voice Agent: Deploy an AI-powered phone agent that answers inbound calls 24/7, captures caller intent, and can transfer calls to your team.
AI Video Meetings: Send a meeting link with an interactive avatar for demos, onboarding, and support.
Automations: Send follow-ups, email documents, and keep a searchable conversation history in your portal.
