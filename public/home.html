Capabilities

AI that doesn’t stop at “chat”.

TalkUSA connects voice + video with the actions your business needs: email, document templates, transfer, and physical mail—built into the same portal.

AI Voice Agent

Answer inbound calls, ask qualifying questions, capture intent, and transfer to your team when needed.

Outbound Dialer

Upload lead lists and let AI agents make outbound calls automatically with personalized scripts.

Video Meetings

Send meeting links instantly and track meeting history—ideal for onboarding, support, and demos.

SMS Messaging

Send SMS messages during or after calls—meeting links, confirmations, or custom follow‑ups.

Payment Collection

Create and send payment links via Square or Stripe. Track payment status in real‑time with webhooks.

Email + Documents

Send follow‑ups with generated documents using your own .docx templates and placeholders.

Physical Mail

Transfer + Routing

Set default transfer destinations and agent‑level overrides for clean handoffs to humans.

History + Analytics

Review calls, meetings, payments, and all actions in your dashboard with charts and searchable records.

Workflow

A simple loop: receive, assist, deliver.

Use your numbers, AI agents, meeting links, payments, and outbound tools as one connected system.

Step 1

Receive or Reach Out

Customers call your number, or your AI dials out from lead lists. Route traffic to the right agent.

Step 2

Assist + Collect

The AI agent handles the conversation, collects payments, and escalates to humans when needed.

Step 3

Deliver

Send follow‑ups: SMS, email documents, meeting links, payment receipts, or physical mail—automatically.
