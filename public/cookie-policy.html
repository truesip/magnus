Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how TalkUSA.NET ("TalkUSA", "we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our websites or use our online Services. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or function more efficiently, as well as to provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of our websites and Services. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, secure login, and access control. You cannot opt out of these cookies because the Services will not function properly without them.

2.2 Performance and analytics cookies

We may use analytics cookies (for example, from tools such as Google Analytics or similar services) to understand how visitors use our websites, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate between pages. This information helps us improve our content and user experience. These cookies typically collect aggregated, pseudonymous information.

2.3 Functionality cookies

These cookies allow our sites to remember your preferences and settings (such as language or region) to provide a more personalized experience.

2.4 Advertising or marketing cookies

At present, TalkUSA does not use third‑party advertising networks on our customer portal. If we introduce such cookies in the future, we will update this Policy and, where required, request your consent.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account and maintain session security.
  • Remember your preferences, such as language or display settings.
  • Monitor site performance and diagnose technical issues.
  • Generate aggregated statistics about how our Services are used.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually configure your browser to block or delete cookies, or to alert you when a website sets cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our websites and may prevent you from logging in or using certain features.

For more information about managing cookies, you can refer to your browser's help documentation or visit websites such as allaboutcookies.org.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting that signals to websites that you do not want your online activities to be tracked. At this time, our websites do not respond to DNT signals. We may revisit this policy as standards evolve.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will post the updated Policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our websites or Services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support@talkusa.net.
